What This Blog is For

Ready...Set...Action

Everyone can use a little motivation here and there. This page is meant to do just that. I motivate my self daily to keep me going. Watching videos, reading articles or just talking to people about my goals and interest. I find myself having many goals and many interest...one just won't do. If I am capable of doing it and have the opportunity and resources to acheive it, then you better believe that I am going to go get it. The first thing we have to do is take a step. Words without action is next to meaningless when it comes to achieving your goals. I have found it more effective to do at least one task a day that will help me get closer to my goal.

Meka


The contents of this website will include blogs on these topics: Health and Fitness, Autodidactism and Formal Education, Motivation and Good Deeds. Content will be updated as often as I can put it up, most likely once or twice a week. It may seem like a bunch of random topics, but I'm sure each topic will resonate within someone. Take what you need and leave what you dont. The blogs are meant to encourage, uplift, inspire and motivate people to get up and take action towards their goals....If you have a dream, go get it. PERIOD!
